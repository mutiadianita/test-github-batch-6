About Cozy Beans Coffee Shop

Welcome to Cozy Beans, your neighborhood coffee haven! Since opening our doors in 2022, we've been dedicated to serving the finest coffee, delicious pastries, and a warm, inviting atmosphere for all.

Our Story

Founded by passionate coffee lovers, Cozy Beans was born from a dream to create a space where everyone can relax, connect, and enjoy quality brews. We source our beans from sustainable farms and roast them locally to ensure every cup is fresh and flavorful.

What We Offer

  • Specialty coffees and teas
  • Freshly baked pastries and snacks
  • Cozy seating and free Wi-Fi
  • Friendly baristas and a welcoming vibe
